FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've answered the most common ones below.

Is the first consultation free?
+
Yes! Your first consultation is complementary. We'll examine your teeth, answer your questions, and discuss your treatment options at no charge.
How do I know which treatment I need?
+
During your consultation, our dentist will perform a comprehensive examination, review any X-rays, and explain all suitable options clearly — so you can make an informed decision.
Do you treat children?
+
Absolutely! We have a dedicated pediatric dentistry team that specialises in making children feel at ease. We recommend bringing kids for their first visit by age 3.
How long does a teeth whitening session take?
+
Our in-office laser whitening takes about 60–90 minutes and can lighten your teeth by up to 8 shades in a single visit.
Are dental implants painful?
+
Most patients experience minimal discomfort during the procedure thanks to local anesthesia. Post-operative soreness is mild and managed with over-the-counter pain relief.
Do you offer EMI or payment plans?
+
Yes! We offer flexible EMI options on major treatments including implants, orthodontics, and smile makeovers. Ask our front desk for details.
