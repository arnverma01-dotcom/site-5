Who We Are

A Legacy of Trusted Dental Excellence Since 1999

Royal Dental Hospital was founded with a singular vision — to make world-class dental care accessible, comfortable, and affordable. Since 1999, we have served tens of thousands of patients from all walks of life with compassion, expertise, and cutting-edge technology.

Our multidisciplinary team of specialists brings together expertise in every branch of modern dentistry, ensuring that whatever your oral health need, you receive the absolute best care under one roof.

Our Journey

25 Years of Milestones

From a single chair clinic to a full multi-specialty hospital — here's how far we've come.

1999

Royal Dental Founded

Dr. Rajesh Kumar opened the clinic with two treatment chairs and a vision to provide affordable, quality dental care.

2005

Digital X-Ray Adoption

First clinic in the region to adopt full digital radiography, reducing patient radiation exposure by 90%.

2010

Implant Centre Launched

Opened a dedicated dental implant wing with internationally trained surgeons and 3D CBCT scanning technology.

2016

ISO Certification Achieved

Earned ISO 9001:2015 certification, setting a new standard for quality management in dental care.

2020

Laser Dentistry Unit

Introduced laser dentistry for soft-tissue procedures, offering patients faster healing and near-painless treatment.

2024

6,400+ Patients Served

Celebrated a milestone of 6,400+ successful dental services with a 98% patient satisfaction rate.
